Kristal All

Fiber artist in Conroe, Texas


Follow me on TikTok!

I'm Kristal. I started learning how to loom knit afghans in the summer of 2021 and I've made over half a dozen blankets since. I started learning to crochet about 6 months ago. So far, I've made a few pumpkins and a Christmas tree skirt. I am working on more blankets and have started attempting to make a sweater.

In December I started learning how to knit with regular knitting needles. I've got quite a stockpile of yarn and have several project ideas. These days I have fallen into the common crafting habit of making too many things at once; I personally don't see this as a bad thing as long as some of my projects get completed every once in a while.
